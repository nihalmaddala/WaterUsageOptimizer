About Miraqua

Miraqua is a smart irrigation assistant built for modern farmers. Using real-time weather, crop-specific science, and machine learning, Miraqua delivers precision watering plans that save water, reduce costs, and promote healthy crop growth — automatically.

Smart Water Optimizer

Our AI-powered 5-day planner generates a custom irrigation schedule tailored to your land size, crop type, and local weather. Just enter your ZIP code, crop, and area — Miraqua calculates the most efficient watering strategy for your field.
